Methodology. This tracker was built using primary legislative and regulatory sources. Each jurisdiction entry links directly to the official law, bill, or regulator page. Jurisdiction coverage on social media age verification issues was cross-referenced against the Tech Policy Press Global Social Media Age Restriction Tracker. Litigation data draws on the NetChoice Litigation Center. Age verification legislation coverage draws on the AVPA State Laws Tracker. The tracker is updated weekly via an automated process that monitors legislative and regulatory developments, court decisions, and commentary.
